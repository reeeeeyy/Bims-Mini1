Location

Barangay KodeGo is a peninsula that is geographically located 8 nautical miles across the bay at 12o 34.56’ latitude, and 120o 54.02’ longitude. It is bounded on the West and Southwest by Dimahanap City, on the Northeast by KungSanSan Bay, on the Southeast by DikoLam Bay and on the South by the Municipality of NovoPringles. It is located at the northern tip of the Province of Malakases and lies approximately 34 kilometers southwest of Idle Province.
