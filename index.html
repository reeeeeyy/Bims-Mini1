About Us

Barangay Profile

Barangay KodeGo (BKG) is considered a newly created barangay of the City of Taguig by virtue of the Sangguniang Panlungsod Ordinance No. XX Series of 20XX. BKG started to function on April 04, 20XX under the following first appointed barangay officials: Joseph Malundas as Barangay Chairman, with Christian Halili, Gail Dan Aykroyd Diaz, and Reynaldo Libor as Barangay Kagawads.

The First Barangay and SK Election was held on October 25, 20XX, and the first set of elected officials took office on December 1, 20XX. The newly elected officials were led by Barangay Joseph Malundas. BKG was able to establish several projects and programs that had a significant impact on the Barangay. Under his term, he was able to build the Barangay Hall, daycare centers, the health center in Sitio X, and the multi-awarded Materials Recovery Facility (MRF).
