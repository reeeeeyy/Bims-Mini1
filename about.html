ABOUT BARANGAY KODEGO

Mission

Barangay KodeGo is dedicated to performing its duties and responsibilities in implementing plans, programs, and policies through providing excellent service to meet the basic needs of its citizens and produce progressive changes that will create a positive impact in the community.

Vission

Barangay KodeGo is a progressive and sustainable barangay built by the collaboration between its citizens and officials to create better living conditions for every individual residing in the community.
