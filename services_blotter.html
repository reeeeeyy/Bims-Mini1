Blotter Records

Disputes can arise among residents or community members in a barangay that may require resolution through the barangay justice system. Your presentation on in-trial, ongoing, resolved, and pending cases could provide an overview of the current status and trends of disputes within your barangay. Here are some key contextual elements that could be included.

Pending Cases

These may include cases that are awaiting further action, cases that are pending documentation or paperwork, or cases that have been temporarily postponed or deferred.

In-Trial Cases

These are cases that have been filed and are currently undergoing the formal dispute resolution process, which may involve mediation, conciliation, or arbitration.

On-going Cases

These may include cases that are currently being investigated, cases that are awaiting mediation or conciliation, or cases that are in the process of documentation or evidence gathering.

Resolved Cases

These are cases that have been settled, mediated, or otherwise resolved through the barangay's efforts, resulting in an agreed-upon resolution between the parties involved.
